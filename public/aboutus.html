About Us

APS World Wide Courier is an international courier and forwarding service provider company. It was established as channel partner for providing shipping solutions and delivering worldwide on behalf of both individual and commercial customers. We provide a complete range of express solutions by offering air freight for export & Import for door as well as port delivery, and door to door courier service to almost all destinations in the world.

APS World Wide Courier is your trusted partner in reliable shipping solutions. With a passion for excellence and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we strive to redefine the standards of the logistics industry.

Our success is largely based on our dedication to providing economical express deliveries along with high levels of customer service.

We are always looking for ways to improve our customers' experience.

What Sets Us Apart:

  • Reliability: With us, you can rest assured that your shipments are in safe hands. Our track record of on-time deliveries and customer satisfaction speaks for itself.

  • Innovation: We embrace technology and innovation to streamline the shipping process and enhance the customer experience.

  • Customer Focus: At the heart of everything we do is our commitment to customer satisfaction. We believe in building long-lasting relationships based on trust, transparency, and personalized service.
